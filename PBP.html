About Palworld

Palworld is an action-adventure, survival, and monster-taming game developed and published by Pocketpair. Set in an expansive open world, players encounter and capture creatures known as "Pals" to assist in various activities such as base building, combat, and exploration. The game offers both single-player and multiplayer modes, supporting up to 32 players on a single server. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Key Features:

  • Genre: Action-Adventure, Survival, Monster-Taming
  • Platforms: Windows, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S, PlayStation 5, macOS
  • Game Modes: Single-player, Multiplayer (up to 32 players)

Gameplay Overview:

In Palworld, players traverse diverse environments, capturing Pals to aid in combat, resource gathering, and construction. The game introduces unique mechanics, allowing Pals to be equipped with weapons and assigned tasks at player-built bases. The blend of whimsical creature collection with survival elements offers a fresh take on the genre. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Online Experience:

  • Multiplayer: Collaborate or compete with up to 32 players in shared servers.
  • Cooperative Play: Team up with friends to tackle challenges and build expansive bases.
  • Community Engagement: Participate in events and share creations with a growing player community.

Popularity & Impact:

Since its early access release in January 2024, Palworld has achieved significant success, selling over 15 million units and attracting a global player base exceeding 32 million. Its unique combination of creature collection and survival mechanics has garnered widespread attention and acclaim. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
