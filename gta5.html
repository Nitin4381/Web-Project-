About GTA V

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is an action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It is set in the fictional state of San Andreas and follows the lives of three criminals as they commit heists while under pressure from a government agency.

Key Features:

  • Genre: Open-world action-adventure
  • Platform: PC, PlayStation, Xbox
  • Game Modes: Story Mode, GTA Online

Gameplay Overview:

GTA V combines driving, shooting, exploration, and missions in a massive open world. Players can switch between three protagonists—Michael, Trevor, and Franklin—each with unique abilities and stories.

Online Experience:

  • GTA Online: An evolving multiplayer universe with heists, businesses, races, and more.
  • Custom Content: Players can create missions, races, and own various properties.

Popularity & Impact:

One of the best-selling video games of all time, GTA V remains popular due to its immersive world and constant online updates.
